# Japan Stay - Fall 2026: Overview

## Scope

This wiki supports planning for a trip to Japan in Fall 2026. It synthesizes travel articles, guides, and research into an interlinked knowledge base covering destinations, logistics, culture, and practical tips.

## Destinations Under Investigation

### Karuizawa, Nagano Prefecture
A mountain resort town at 1,000m elevation, less than 2 hours from Tokyo via Hokuriku Shinkansen. Strong candidate for a 2-3 day autumn excursion. Key draws include autumn foliage (Kumobaike Pond, Usui Pass), historic sites (Former Mikasa Hotel, Nakasendo Route), onsen (Hoshino Onsen Tombo-no-yu), boutique shopping (Harunire Terrace, Old Karuizawa Ginza Street), and outlet shopping (Karuizawa Prince Shopping Plaza).

## Key Themes

- **Autumn foliage** — timing visits to coincide with peak koyo at various elevations
- **Shinkansen accessibility** — destinations reachable as day trips or short stays from a Tokyo base
- **Onsen and relaxation** — hot spring experiences integrated into itineraries
- **Historic routes** — the Nakasendo and other Edo-period trails as cultural/hiking activities
- **Accommodation** — vacation rental options (villas, cottages), pricing seasonality, and the new Karuizawa accommodation tax (effective June 2026)
- **Dining** — Shinshu Wagyu, handmade soba, French cuisine, and seasonal highland produce across three main dining zones
